🧠 Artificial Intelligence

What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines. It involves learning (acquiring information), reasoning (using rules to reach conclusions), and self-correction.

Key Concepts

  • Machine Learning (ML): Algorithms that improve automatically through experience.
  • Neural Networks: Computing systems inspired by the biological neural networks of animal brains.
  • NLP: Natural Language Processing allows computers to understand text and speech.
