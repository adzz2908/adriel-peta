🎨 Visual Graphics & Design

2D Design & UI/UX

User Interface (UI) design focuses on the look and layout. User Experience (UX) focuses on how it feels to use. Tools like Figma and Adobe XD are industry standards.

3D Modeling

Creating 3D objects for games and movies. The most popular free tool is Blender.

  • Modeling: Creating the shape (mesh).
  • Rigging: Adding "bones" to move the mesh.
  • Rendering: Turning the 3D scene into a 2D image.

Color Theory

Understanding how colors mix and match is crucial. Complementary colors (opposites on the wheel) create high contrast and impact.
